About The Event

The India eMobility Show is a premier event aimed at positioning India’s EV market at the centre of global dialogue and promoting the adoption of EVs in the country. Building on the resounding success of the first edition, the event will once again provide an ideal platform to network, exchange ideas, and form long-term partnerships.

CONFERENCE

The conference at the India eMobility Show will host the best minds from the industry including policymakers, business leaders, investors, entrepreneurs and other key stakeholders from across the world.

EXHIBITION

India eMobility Show will offer exhibitors a cutting edge platform to showcase the latest EV models, innovative solutions, and next-gen technologies in front of thousands of industry professionals.

Market Outlook

India's mobility sector is at the brink of an electric revolution. The stringent Greenhouse gas (GHG) emission norms, generous subsidies and tax benefits to encourage EV adoption, and the price sensitive population have resulted in the growth for electric transportation in the country.
